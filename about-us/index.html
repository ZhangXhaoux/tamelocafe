About Us

WHO WE ARE

A Javanese culinary haven with flavors that dance on your palate. Crafting authentic Indonesian delights since 1945. Savor our traditional recipes and experience the magic of Java's rich heritage.

Our passion for Javanese cuisine runs as deep as Java’s cultural roots. Every dish we serve is a tribute to my grandmother’s kitchen, where I first fell in love with these flavors. At Dhaharan, we’re not just serving food; we’re sharing our heritage, one plate at a time.

ANDRE Sutino - FOUNDER
